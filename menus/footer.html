George Gadanidis spends 50-60 days each year in elementary school classrooms, collaborating with teachers to design better ways of engaging children with mathematics. He is a professor and researcher of mathematics education at Western University.

The Faculty of Education, at Western University, is committed to mathematics education. See our new Masters in Professional Education - Mathematics Education, a fully online, 2-year program.

The Fields Institute for Research in Mathematical Sciences is an on-going supporter of outreach programs in mathematics education.
